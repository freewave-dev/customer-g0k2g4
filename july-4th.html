July 4th · Independence Day

Celebrate the 4th of July at Rosario!

Join us for an unforgettable Independence Day celebration at Rosario, featuring great food, live music, waterfront views, and a spectacular fireworks display over Cascade Bay!

🎆 Fireworks Show

Begins at dusk — approximately 10:00–10:30 PM.

🍔 The Blackfish Café at The Beach House

Hours: 8:00 AM until after the fireworks.

Spend the evening on The Lawn, where you'll enjoy food, drinks, and live entertainment.

🎵 Live Music: Wild Child performs beginning at 8:00 PM.

Grab dinner, sip a drink, and relax on The Lawn before the fireworks begin. Please note that all open beverages must remain within the designated lawn area. No outside food or beverages are permitted in this area.

Picnic Tables & Seating on The Lawn: Seating is first come, first serve — feel free to bring a picnic blanket to hang out on in The Lawn.

Serving to-go food and beverages!

🍽 The Harbor Bar & Grill

Hours: 12:00 PM until after the fireworks.

Join us for lunch or dinner before the show! Reservations are recommended.

Make a reservation by calling or texting (360) 776-9141 — or visit harborbarorcas.com.

All open beverages must remain within the designated lawn area. No outside food or beverages are permitted in this area.

🌅 Public Fireworks Viewing at the Marina View Lawn

This is the lawn located between The Blackfish Café and The Harbor Bar & Grill. It will be open to the public for fireworks viewing.

Bring a picnic blanket or lawn chair and enjoy one of the best views on the island. This space is first come, first serve.

We encourage guests to order food & beverages to-go from The Blackfish Café and enjoy them from their viewing spot.

🚗 Parking Information

Parking at Rosario is extremely limited. Please:

  • Follow all parking signs, traffic cones, and staff directions.
  • Park only in designated areas. Vehicles parked illegally are subject to towing.
  • Carpool whenever possible.
  • Consider taking the shuttle from Eastsound.

🚌 Shuttle Service

Scenic Shuttle will provide continuous service between Eastsound and Rosario from 8:00 PM to 11:30 PM.

Cost: $10 per person, each way (cash or Venmo).

Pickup Location: Island Market (by the flagpole) in Eastsound.
