Welcome to the Harbor

A tiki bar and grill on the edge of the Salish Sea

We sit right on Rosario Marina, where the dock meets a covered outdoor kitchen and a wide cedar deck looks out over the boats. Think bright party-shirt energy with serious cooking — Penn Cove mussels, ceviche done Peruvian-style, tropical drinks that take their time, and a steak you'll talk about on the ferry ride home.

Tie up at the dock, hike in from Moran State Park, taxi over from town, or splash down in a Kenmore Air seaplane. However you arrive, there's a seat in the sun waiting.

  • Waterfront Dining. Open air seating above Rosario Harbor.
  • Pacific Seafood. Local oysters, mussels, rockfish, salmon.
  • Tiki Bar. Hurricanes, daiquiris, and rum flights at sunset.

Getting Here

Drive, sail, hike, or fly in

Tucked into Rosario Harbor on the east side of Orcas Island, we're easy to reach by every form of Pacific Northwest transport.

Address 1424 Rosario Road · Eastsound, WA · United States

By Car

Free parking is available for guests. Need a ride? Three Orcas taxi services serve the harbor:

  • Scenic Shuttle(306) 298-8414
  • Keith's Taxi(360) 298-1639
  • Orcas Island Taxi(360) 376-8294

By Boat

Free* day-use moorage at Rosario Harbor is available for guests dining at the Harbor Bar. Purchase parking online at rosarioharbor.com and bring your receipt.

*Vessels up to 30': $25 parking is reimbursed on receipts of $25+. For 30–50' vessels: $40 parking is reimbursed on receipts of $75+.

By Foot

Camping at Moran State Park? The Harbor Bar is just a 1.25-mile walk from the North End campground (1.5 mi from South End, 2 mi from Midway). Hike around Cascade Lake, follow the spillway down to the harbor — about 360 ft of elevation change.

By Seaplane

Rosario Harbor is a scheduled stop for Kenmore Air and Friday Harbor Seaplanes. Have your own floatplane? Call the Marina at (360) 472-4866.
